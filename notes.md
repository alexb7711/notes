# Estimation
[Estimation](estimation.md) is the process of attempting to derive the true value of measurements taken from a system that are subect to noise and/or bias.

## Optimal Estimation
[Optimal Estimation](optimal_estimation.md), much like estimation, attempts to estimate the true value from a measurement that has been subject to noise and bias. This optimization method is known as the [Kalman Filter](kalman_filter.md).
