:PROPERTIES:

** Optimal Estimation
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: optimal-estimation
:END:
[[file:optimal_estimation.html][Optimal Estimation]], much like estimation, attempts to estimate the true value from a measurement that has been subject
to noise and bias. This optimization method is known as the [[file:kalman_filter.html][Kalman Filter]].
