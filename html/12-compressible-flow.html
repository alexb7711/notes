Oblique Shocks

There are three angles of interest:

  • : The angle from the centreline of the nose to the edge of the object (wedge angle).
  • : The wave angle. The angle from the centreline of the nose of the object to the outer "line" of the shock wave.
  • : The deflection angle of the surrounding fluid. This angle is equal to half of the wedge angle.
