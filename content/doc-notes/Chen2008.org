* Introduction
- The constant current, constant voltage method was implemented to overcome the problem of requiring a 100% precise full
  charge detector
-Under the CC–CV charge strategy, a CC is applied first until the battery voltage increases to a preset voltage (i.e.,
 battery full charged voltage). This CV is held after reaching the preset voltage with the charge current automatically
 reduced. When the charge current reduces to zero, the battery is 100% fully charged.
