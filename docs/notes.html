3. Estimation

Estimation is the process of attempting to derive the true value of measurements taken from a system that are subect to noise and/or bias.

3.1. Optimal Estimation

Optimal Estimation, much like estimation, attempts to estimate the true value from a measurement that has been subject to noise and bias. This optimization method is known as the Kalman Filter.
